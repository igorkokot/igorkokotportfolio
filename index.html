About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

I'm an acoustic engineer with post-production experience in audio and video across multiple domains.

Audio:
- Original music composition
- Electronic music mixing and mastering
- Voice-over post-production
- Movie soundtrack composition

Video:
- Video editing and post-production
- Music video editing
- Collaborative filmmaking with the bezuad collective

I approach every project with enthusiasm and creativity, delivering high quality work to meet my clients' needs. Feel free to explore my portfolio and contact me if you want to work together!
