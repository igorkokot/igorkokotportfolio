About me

Hi, I’m Igor Kokot, and I specialize in audio production and post-production. I create house and downtempo music with a melancholic feel, sometimes leaning into darker tones, as well as composing soundtracks for movies.
Professionally, I focus on video editing and audio post-production for educational videos, enhancing clarity, balance, and overall sound quality through precise processing and restoration techniques. Beyond audio, I also edit short-form videos, including music videos and YouTube videos, tailoring the style and pacing to fit each project’s unique needs.
Having worked with numerous clients across both fields, I approach every collaboration with creativity. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to work together.
